## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the homepage and click "Sign Up"
2. Choose your account type: Investor or Startup
3. Complete the authentication process
4. You'll be redirected to the onboarding flow

### Onboarding

#### For Investors:
1. **Profile Information**
   - Investment focus and strategy
   - Accreditation status
   - LinkedIn profile (optional)
   - Professional bio

2. **Investment Preferences**
   - Minimum ROI threshold
   - Preferred startup stages (Pre-seed, Seed, Series A, etc.)
   - Sector interests (Technology, Healthcare, Finance, etc.)
   - Geographic preferences

#### For Startups:
1. **Company Information**
   - Company name and logo
   - Industry and stage
   - Company description
   - Website URL

2. **Additional Details**
   - Founded year
   - Team size
   - Location
   - Funding history

## For Investors

### Discovering Startups

1. **Browse Campaigns**
   - Navigate to the "Discover" page
   - View all published campaigns
   - Use filters to narrow down options:
     - Industry
     - Stage
     - Fundraising amount
     - Search by keywords

2. **AI-Powered Recommendations**
   - Visit "Recommendations" in your dashboard
   - Get personalized matches based on your preferences
   - View match scores and AI reasoning
   - See risk analysis for each opportunity

### Making an Investment

1. **Review Campaign Details**
   - Watch the video pitch (VSL)
   - Download and review the pitch deck
   - Check team information
   - Review use of funds and risks

2. **Investment Process**
   - Click "Invest Now"
   - Enter investment amount (must be within min/max range)
   - Review terms and equity offered
   - Complete Stripe checkout
   - Wait for startup approval

3. **Investment Status**
   - **Pending**: Awaiting startup review
   - **Accepted**: Investment confirmed, funds in escrow
   - **Rejected**: Investment declined, refund processed

### Managing Your Portfolio

1. **Dashboard Overview**
   - Total invested amount
   - Active investments
   - Pending reviews
   - Portfolio breakdown by industry and stage

2. **Investment Tracking**
   - View all investments in "My Investments"
   - Track status updates
   - Receive email notifications
   - Monitor campaign progress

- 3 investments per month
- Basic analytics
- Email support

**Pro Plan ($149/month):**
- Unlimited investments
- Advanced analytics
- AI-powered recommendations
- Priority support

## For Startups

### Creating a Campaign

1. **Campaign Basics**
   - Campaign title (clear and compelling)
   - Detailed description (minimum 100 characters)
   - Industry and stage
   - Fundraising goal

2. **Investment Terms**
   - Minimum investment amount
   - Maximum investment amount
   - Equity offered (percentage)
   - Campaign deadline

3. **Marketing Materials**
   - Video Sales Letter (VSL) URL
   - Pitch deck upload (PDF)
   - Use of funds breakdown
   - Risk disclosure
   - Team information

4. **Publishing**
   - Review all information
   - Click "Publish Campaign"

### Managing Investments

1. **Review Investment Offers**
   - Navigate to "Investment Offers"
   - View pending investments
   - See investor information
   - Review investment amounts

2. **Accept or Reject**
   - **Accept**: Funds transferred to escrow, investor notified

   - Decision should be made within 7 days

3. **Dashboard Metrics**
   - Total raised
   - Active campaigns
   - Number of investors
   - Funding trends over time
   - Campaign performance

### Best Practices

   - Offer competitive equity terms

2. **Investor Relations**

## Investment Process

### How It Works

1. **Discovery**
   - Investors browse campaigns or receive recommendations
   - Review campaign details and materials

2. **Investment**
   - Investor submits investment through platform
   - Payment processed via Stripe
   - Funds held in escrow

3. **Review**
   - Startup reviews investment offer
   - Decision made within 7 days
   - Investor notified of decision

4. **Completion**
   - If accepted: Funds transferred, equity documented
   - If rejected: Automatic refund processed

### Payment Security

- All payments processed through Stripe
- PCI-DSS compliant
- Funds held in escrow until acceptance
- Automatic refunds for rejected investments

### Fees

- **Investors**: No platform fees (only Stripe processing fees)
- **Startups**: 5% success fee on accepted investments
- **Subscriptions**: Monthly recurring billing

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is HEBED AI available internationally?**
A: Currently available in the US. International expansion planned.

**Q: How secure is my data?**
A: We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. All payment data is handled by Stripe.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, anytime. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

### For Investors

**Q: What happens if a startup rejects my investment?**
A: You'll receive a full refund within 5-7 business days.

**Q: Can I invest in multiple campaigns?**
A: Yes, subject to your subscription plan limits.

**Q: How are recommendations generated?**
A: Using AI analysis of your preferences, investment history, and campaign data.

**Q: What is accreditation status?**
A: Accredited investors meet SEC requirements for income/net worth. Non-accredited investors have investment limits.

### For Startups

**Q: How long does it take to get funded?**
A: Depends on your campaign and investor interest. Most campaigns see activity within 2-4 weeks.

**Q: Can I edit my campaign after publishing?**
A: Contact support for major changes. Minor updates can be made in dashboard.

**Q: What happens to rejected investment funds?**
A: Automatically refunded to the investor. No action needed from you.

**Q: How do I receive funds?**
A: Funds transferred to your bank account after investor acceptance and escrow release.

### Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Not yet. The web platform is mobile-responsive.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Contact support@hebed.ai with details.
